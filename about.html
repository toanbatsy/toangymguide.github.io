Training Styles for pure Hypertrophy

Please learn about this training style prior to your workout First, we have to talk about range of motion. For pure muscle growth, your muscles need to experience a full range of motion. Extend your muscles so that they are fully stretched at the bottom, and then bring the weights up until the muscles are contracted.

The second thing is lifting tempo. Don't just focus on getting the weight up. The lowering phase of the lift is just as important! The lowering phase of a rep should last around 2 to 4 seconds. It doesn't have to be slow, but it has to be controlled. Think about your muscles tearing apart in the negative.

Finally, we need to talk about our excersise selection. You need to choose excersises that allow you experience a full range of motion. That said, you need a deep stretch at the bottom and a good contraction at the top.

Why learning about training styles? Sure, you can get away with poor form and technique in training. But I doubt those gains are optimal. These training styles have been researched and backed by science for a few years now so you can totally trust in these strategies and start applying these techniques into your training.
