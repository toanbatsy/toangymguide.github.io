About my journey

I started lifting when I was in grade 12th. Back then, I didn't love the gym as much as I do now. Whenever I came back from school, my mom took me to the gym on the way home. I was there without a goal, a purpose. I did what I saw everyone was doing. But then, one day my brother showed up in the gym and taught me the true "newbie" phase, my love for the gym started growing. I started to look forward to the soreness the next day after a workout. I started to like the pain the leg press gave me because that it when my mind completely shut off to fully focus on the lift.

I love the gym so much that I promised myself to not miss a day. I love every scream, every grunt I make when I do a huge lift. I started to chase pound after pound, looking forward to increase the weight on every excersise. At first, I could only bench the barbell. But now I can bench 90kg.

I hope that after visiting this website, you'll start fiding your true passion in life. It doesn't have to be the gym. I want you to find something you enjoy doing, something you loving doing it so much that as if the world would collapse if you don't do it.

In this website, you will find training tips, things I wish I knew when I started lifting, and my journey.
